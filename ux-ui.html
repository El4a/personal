Personalized homepage

A unifying project to accommodate our users (Belgian citizens) to find an overview of all their career-related services in one go. Required to be usable by anyone everywhere, meaning a strong emphasis on being super-userfriendly, accessible, and responsive.

Follow-up dashboard

A challenging, classicaly overloaded, legacy application that was due for an overhaul. The main improvement was reducing the length and complexity of the list, changing it into a combination of priority-based "new"-alerts and custom presets to filter.

Design system

For the past six years, I have maintained, extended, and rebuild our current in-house design system. This consists of HTML & Sass files with BEM formatting, compiled by a NPM-Gulp combo into a markdown/handlebars documentation site. Here we document our branding and guidelines as well as standalone components and full code-ready html mockups.

Content pages rework

A collection of legacy pages that were in need of a redesign within the context of the brand styleguide.

Housewarming (side project)

Most likely no longer fully functional (I won't show up with a drink), but still online to take a look.
